About me

Turning data into decisions

I'm a Senior Data Analyst based in Kampala, Uganda, with a Software Engineering background from Makerere University. For the past 5+ years, I've been building the analytics infrastructure that SafeBoda — one of Africa's leading ride-hailing platforms — relies on to make daily business decisions.

"I believe the real value of data work is only realised when insights reach the right people, in a format they can act on."

My work spans the full analytics toolchain — advanced SQL, Python, and AppScript automation pipelines that schedule KPI loads from Metabase into Google Sheets; Tableau, Power BI, Looker, and Superset for BI; PostGIS and ArcGIS for geospatial analysis; CleverTap for app event tracking and campaign analytics; and KoboToolbox for field data collection. I build and automate end-to-end — from data ingestion to executive dashboard.

Outside my day job, I actively build and publish machine learning and analytics projects — a credit risk engine, an automated financial reconciliation pipeline, and an operational delivery analysis system. I'm currently exploring senior analytics and analytics engineering roles, particularly remote opportunities with data-mature companies.
